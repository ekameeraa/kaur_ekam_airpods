AirPods are wireless earbuds designed by Apple Inc. that were first announced on September 7, 2016, alongside the iPhone 71. They are Apple’s entry-level wireless headphones, sold alongside the AirPods Pro and AirPods Max1. The AirPods are equipped with a custom-designed Apple H1 chip that helps optimize battery life and delivers a faster and more stable wireless connection2. The AirPods come with a charging case that provides multiple charges and up to 24 hours of battery life2. The AirPods are compatible with Siri, which allows users to access music, make phone calls, and ask for directions2. The AirPods also feature automatic on/off and automatic pause/play when they are removed from or placed in the ear2.
